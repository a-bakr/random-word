# Faseeh — User Guide

Welcome to **Faseeh** — a calm little space to practice how you speak. No setup, no
sign-up, no clutter. Just tap and start.

---

## The one rule: tap anywhere

The whole screen is a button. **Every tap gives you something new to say out loud.**
That's the entire app. Everything else below just makes practice nicer.

Say each thing out loud, clearly, at your own pace. Then tap for the next one.

---

## The three practice modes

Switch between them using the icons at the **top of the screen**.

| Icon | Mode | What it does |
|------|------|--------------|
| **A** (Type) | **Words** | Gives you one random word at a time to warm up your articulation. Great for a quick daily warm-up. |
| **⚡** (Zap) | **Twisters** | Tongue twisters — full phrases that train your mouth and clarity. Each one **plays audio** so you can hear it first, then copy it. |
| **🔥** (Flame) | **Warm-up** | A guided **20-step vocal warm-up** (breathing, pitch, projection, and more). Follow the picture and instructions, press play to hear the guide, then tap to move on. |

---

## Moving through content

- **Tap** → next word / twister / exercise.
- **Swipe right** → next. **Swipe left** → go back to the previous one.
- In **Twisters** and **Warm-up**, you can also tap the **right side** of the screen to
  go forward and the **left side** to go back.
- **Pull down** from the top → refresh the app.

---

## Record your voice 🎤

Want to hear yourself? You can record any time.

- **Hold your finger anywhere** on the screen (about a second) to **start recording**,
  then **let go** to stop. Or tap the **microphone button** at the bottom-left.
- While recording, you'll see a **live transcript** of what you said near the bottom.
- Your clips appear as a small list at the bottom-left. For each clip you can:
  - **▶ Play** it back,
  - tap the **text** to read the full transcript,
  - **✕** remove it.

> Recordings are kept only for your current session — they disappear if you reload.

## The buttons, explained

### Top of screen
- **Words / Twisters / Warm-up** — switch practice modes (see above).
- **⚙ Settings** — open your preferences.
- **ⓘ About** — about the app and its creator.
- **🌐 Language** — tap to switch language (English ⇄ Arabic). The app flips fully to
  Arabic, including right-to-left layout.

### Bottom of screen
- **a / A** (bottom-center) — make the text **smaller or bigger**. Set it to whatever's
  comfortable to read at arm's length.
- **🎤 Microphone** (bottom-left) — start/stop recording.
- **⏱ Timer** (bottom-right) — a practice timer (default 60 seconds). It starts when you
  begin and resets each tap, helping you pace a session. Tap the clock to turn it on/off.
- **▶ / ⏹ Replay** (Twisters only, bottom-right) — replay or stop the twister's audio.

## Coaching tips 💡

As you practice, small **tip tags** may float near your word. Tap one to open a short
coaching insight — vocal techniques, storytelling frameworks, and speaking styles
(inspired by Vinh Giang's STAGE Academy). Tap **"try now"** to jump straight into
recording yourself practicing it.

You can show more, fewer, or no tips in **Settings → Coaching tips**.

---

## Settings ⚙

- **Theme** — Light or Dark.
- **Sound effects** — the little tap sound, On or Off.
- **Words on screen** — show 1 word at a time, or several at once (up to ∞).
- **Word display** — words appear where you **tap** (Random) or always in the **Center**.
- **Coaching tips** — how many tips to show (Off, 1, 2, or 3).
- **Account** — optionally **sign in with Google** to sync your progress across devices.
  You don't need an account to use Faseeh — it works fully without one.

---

## A simple daily routine

1. Open **Warm-up** and do a few steps to loosen up. 🔥
2. Switch to **Twisters**, listen, and repeat a handful. ⚡
3. Finish in **Words**, recording yourself and playing it back. 🎤

Two or three minutes a day is enough. Speak clearly, breathe, and have fun with it.

---

*That's everything. When in doubt — just tap.* ✨
